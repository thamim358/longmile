Compliances & Accreditations

FACTORY CERTIFICATIONS

We have been recognised with Sedex certifications and standards, indicating our commitment to maintaining service quality and enabling transparency across the supply chain.

Responsible for People

Our factory is audited on a periodic basis in order to maintain our commitment to our clients' compliance needs . We endeavour to eliminate zero-tolerance issues like child and forced labour, discrimination, harassment, and women's rights or any other serious aberrations that come under such scope.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

At Longmile, we believe in business for good. We engage in employee welfare programs including soft skill training, providing health insurance, transportation and supporting the local community.

QUALITY

Longmile Fashions believes in “Quality first”. We are committed to improving quality on a consistent basis and giving our clients the best service possible. We procure the finest raw materials, recruit talented employees, and equip all departments with cutting-edge machinery. Our quality team oversees sample checks, inline, end-line and final inspections. Final inspection is performed according to standard procedures for Acceptable Quality Level (AQL) based on the ISO Standard and has developed a 4-point system to guarantee that each stage of our manufacturing process meets stringent standards.

Our laboratory services check colour matching as per the requirement, weight, dimensional stability, shrinkage, appearance after washing, colour fastness to washing, staining, and rubbing to ensure zero defects.

POWERING OUR SOURCING WITH RENEWABLE ELECTRICITY

As a mark of our dedication to a cleaner and more sustainable future, solar power generates a substantial amount of electricity at our factory. Longmile aims to achieve 95% renewable energy by FY 2027 by adding solar power across our factory and office. Longmile Fashions conducts tree plantation drives to reduce the carbon footprint across the supply chain ensuring a greener tomorrow

Water Conservation

Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP) in supplier dyeing and washing facilities help control and recycle wastewater. We comply with Zero Discharge of Hazardous Chemicals (ZDHC) wastewater requirements for effluent management monitoring and control.

Waste Management

Longmile aims to close the loop on fabric wastage. We reduce and recycle the fabric wastage during cutting.

Supply-Chain Transparency

We have a strong approach to supply-chain transparency, recording every step of the production process to ensure full accountability.
