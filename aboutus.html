Company Profile - Overview

Longmile Fashions Private Limited is an export-oriented knitted apparel supplier with a capacity of 300,000 pieces per month, dedicated to serving our clients through design-driven innovation, vertically integrated operations, and environmentally responsible business practices.

With quality as the core, our highly skilled team of professionals create and deliver the precise, high-quality product that the customer demands, on time.

Moreover, we maintain a tight relationship with the market and stay abreast of the latest trend, products, and high-street needs. Strategically located near Tirupur – the centre of India's knitwear exports.
